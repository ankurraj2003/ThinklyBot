# Custom AI Agents for Your Workflows
We build reasoning and operational agents that integrate seamlessly into your operations - combining intelligence for complex decisions with reliability for repetitive work.

## Two types of agents, one seamless system
1. **Reasoning Agents:** Use advanced models to analyze, plan, and make decisions in real time. They handle complex tasks that require judgment, context, and multi-step thinking. (Analyze context & constraints -> Reason through options -> Execute intelligent decision)
2. **Operational Agents:** Execute predefined workflows reliably and efficiently. They run on schedules or triggers, handling repetitive tasks with consistency and audit trails. 

## Agents we build
* **Deep Research Agents:** Investigate topics across multiple sources, synthesize findings, resolve contradictions, and deliver comprehensive reports with citations.
* **Knowledge Search Agents:** Query internal knowledge bases and external sources, rank results by relevance, and deliver accurate answers with supporting documentation.
* **Conversational Agents:** Maintain context across dialogues, clarify intent, and orchestrate workflows while staying on-brand and compliant.
* **Data Analysis Agents:** Explore datasets, generate queries, run statistical analysis, and explain insights with visualizations and recommendations.
* **Content Generation Agents:** Create platform-specific content, adapt tone to audience, and incorporate structured data while following brand guidelines.
* **Evaluation Agents:** Score outputs against policies or quality rubrics, flag issues, and recommend specific improvements.

# Turn Documents into Knowledge
We build intelligent knowledge systems that transform scattered documents, wikis, and data into searchable knowledge bases and connected knowledge graphs - surfacing insights your teams can actually use.

## Two approaches to knowledge management
1. **Knowledge Bases:** Transform documents, wikis, and internal data into searchable knowledge bases with semantic search and relevance ranking.
2. **Knowledge Graphs:** Map relationships between entities, uncover hidden connections, and navigate complex information networks with visual knowledge graphs.

## Why Knowledge Infrastructure Matters
* **Stop losing institutional knowledge:** When people leave, their expertise shouldn't disappear with them.
* **Accelerate onboarding:** New hires get instant access to tribal knowledge instead of playing detective.
* **Make better decisions faster:** Leaders can query historical context and related work before making calls.
* **Reduce repetitive questions:** Teams find answers themselves instead of interrupting others.

## Capabilities we deploy
* **Document Processing:** Ingest and parse PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, and unstructured data with automatic chunking, metadata extraction, and format normalization.
* **Semantic Search:** Find information based on meaning, not keywords. Rank results by relevance using embeddings and return precise answers with source citations.
* **Q&A Systems:** Answer natural language questions from your knowledge base with context-aware responses, source attribution, and confidence scoring.
* **Multi-source Integration:** Combine data from Confluence, Notion, SharePoint, Google Drive, and databases into a unified, searchable knowledge layer.
